# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We expect contributors to communicate clearly, debate ideas in good faith, and keep criticism focused on the work instead of the person.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Respect different technical backgrounds and levels of experience
- Give concrete, actionable feedback
- Assume good intent and ask for clarification before escalating
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project for users

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Personal attacks, harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Dismissive behavior toward basic questions or newcomer mistakes
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

If you need to report a conduct issue, open a private security-style report to the maintainers instead of discussing it publicly.
